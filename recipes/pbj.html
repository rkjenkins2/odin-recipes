Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich


The GOAT of school lunch!

No fancy schmancy here. Just the OG of afternoon snacks.


Ingredients:

  • Wonder bread, bunny bread, or something equally cheap, soft, and white
  • Peter Pan or Skippy peanut butter -- here we don't go cheap!
  • Strawberry jelly

Steps:

  1. Take two slices of delicious white bread and splay them open
  2. Apply peanut butter to one slice
  3. Wipe off all the excess peanut butter from your knife onto the clean slice of bread
  4. Apply jelly to that slice
  5. Stick 'em together
  6. Slice diagonally!
  7. Watch some cartoons, eat some doritos, and enjoy!
