Sous Vide Filet Mignon

The tastiest, easiest filet you can make

There is no simpler way to get a tasty filet with the perfect texture and rareness than using a sous vide circulator!


Ingredients:

  • Two 3-4 oz filets
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Bring pot to 128 degrees farenheit
  2. Season filets with salt and pepper
  3. Add filets to one gallon ziploc bag and add a tbsp of oil
  4. Use the submersion method to lock out any air from the bag
  5. When the water is ready, submerge for 90 minutes.
  6. After 90 minutes, remove filets from Ziploc, pat dry with a paper towel.
  7. Heat oiled cast iron with remaining vegetable oil
  8. Once sizzling, add filets and sear each side for 30-60 seconds
  9. Slice on cool cutting board and enjoy!
