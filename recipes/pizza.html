Margherita Pizza with Burrata

You'll never have regular mozzarella again!

My first experience with burrata on a margherita pizza instead of your regular, everyday mozzarella was at Olio Pizza in Los Angeles. Once you experience this, you can never go back! Here is my simple homemade version using all Trader Joe's ingredients.


Ingredients:

  • One frozen wood-fire dough
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Pizza sauce
  • Burrata cheese
  • Fresh grated parm reggiano
  • Fresh shredded mozzarella
  • Campari tomatoes
  • Black garlic flakes
  • Himalayan sea salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven.
  2. Massage olive oil into the dough
  3. Add pizza sauce to dough
  4. Slice campari tomatoes and add to dough
  5. Sprinkle fresh mozzarella and parmesan reggiano
  6. Cook in oven at 400 for 8 minutes
  7. Remove pizza from oven, add fresh basil and burrata
  8. Sprinkle additional olive oil as needed
  9. Return to oven for 30 seconds
  10. Remove from oven, add salt and fresh grated cheese as needed
  11. Mangia!
